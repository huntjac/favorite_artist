Juice WRLD

Juice WRLD (Born Jarad Higgins) is an american rapper and singer. Born in 1998 in Chicago, Illinois, his parents had divorced when he was 3, causing him to grow up with his mother. He also has an older brother. His mother was very religious and conservative and did not let him listen to hip-hop growing up. He started rapping in his sophomore year at highschool.

He rose to fame in music when he released his hit song "All Girls are the Same". The song entered the ‘Billboard’ chart, and debuted at number 92, on the ‘Billboard Hot 100.
